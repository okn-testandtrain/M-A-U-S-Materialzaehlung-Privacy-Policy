# Privacy Policy for M-A-U-S Materialzählung

**Last Updated:** March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how **M-A-U-S Materialzählung** ("the App") handles data. This app is a professional utility designed for internal inventory management and barcode scanning.

## 1. Overview
The App is intended for internal company use to facilitate material counting and inventory updates. It connects to a private Odoo instance to synchronize stock levels.

## 2. Permissions & Data Collection
* **Camera:** The App requires access to your device camera exclusively for **barcode scanning** (Location IDs and Material/Item IDs). No photos, videos, or facial data are captured, stored, or shared.
* **Internet Access:** The App uses an internet connection to communicate with our internal **Odoo instance** and to send anonymous usage metrics.
* **Inventory Data:** When a user submits an update, the App transmits the new inventory quantities, material IDs, and location IDs to the Odoo server.

## 3. User Privacy & Identity
* **No Personal Information (PII):** The App does not require a user account or login. We do not collect, store, or transmit names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personally identifiable information.
* **Analytics & Telemetry:** We use **TelemetryDeck** to collect anonymous usage information (e.g., which app features are used). This data is fully anonymized, aggregated, and contains no information that could identify an individual user.

## 4. Data Sharing & Security
* **Internal Sync:** Data is transmitted only to our managed Odoo instance for business operations.
* **No Third-Party Sale:** We do not sell, rent, or share any data with unauthorized third parties or advertising networks.

## 5. Contact
For any questions regarding this privacy policy or the data handled by **M-A-U-S Materialzählung**, please contact the developer via the contact information provided in the Google Play Store listing.
